Про нас

Wo we are

Pole Dance Hub Brasov - is a space for self-expression, realization of art ideas, developement of phisical skills and a place where the human soul starts to dance. Made by pole dancers for everyone who is ready to be obsessed by pole danceing as much as we are!
We are the first pole dancers in beautiful city of Brasov, who geathered and accomodated here more than 12 years of pole dance trainings with performing and teaching experience. Our goal is developing of pole dancing in Transilvania, organization of experience exchange between pole dancers from other parts of Romania and world, and sharing all our skills and knowledge to everyone who is interested in pole dance.

Classes

  • Pole dance

    Pole dance training lasts 60min, consists of warm up part and pole part. Groups have mixed level mostly. All students get different programm during pole dance class, which is up to their level. We learn dance moves around the pole, spins,tricks and combos from simple to advanced.

  • Exotic

    This is the most feminim direction of pole dance. Exotic pole dance training lasts 60min, consists of warm up part and dancing part. Student should be familiar with the pole after at least few classes of pole dance or after any other dancing experience style in the past. All students learn similar dance routine with trainer using the same music composition.

  • Pole kids

    Pole kids classes can become the best hobby for kids from 8 to 14 years. Training length is 50min. Pole programm is based on general phisical trainings. Consists of warm up part and pole part. Learning of pole dance tricks and combos ismixed with acrobatic base which is fun and useful for kids.

  • Stretching

    Stretching classes are directed to increase joint mobility and muscle elasticity. Each training is devoted to separate zone: middle or front splits, back flexibility. training lasts 60min and contains warming up part and stretching part.
